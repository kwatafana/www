CORPORATE PROFILE



Enterprise Based Corporate Social Investment (EBCSI)

As a previously disadvantaged enterprise, Wellspring Global is well aware of the need for empowerment of historically disadvantaged individuals and small enterprises. Wellspring Global has a policy that initiates the provision of direct financial, mentorship and coaching assistance as a way of outreach to disadvantaged and well-deserving beneficiaries.

We have developed interventions such as our “Wellspring Business Breakfast” program, given the much needed skills and mentorship required for entrepreneurs and their businesses to grow. This program is to be rolled out across the African continent as a means of empowering individuals to manage and grow their businesses in partnership with willing corporate institutions and investors.

Our Vision

To be a true Pan-African Investment Solutions Advisor and Provider; seeking to grow the performance of business entities, increase investment opportunities, and contribute to the development of sustainable economies.

Skills Development

Wellspring Global provides development opportunities such as on-the-job mentorship in partnership with our clients as part of our commitment to skills development. Wellspring Global continuously ensures that key operational staff needs are directed to locals to ensure skills transfer as well as policy advocacy in procurement from local businesses as much as possible.

Our Mission

To actively participate as an expert solutions provider in the enhancement of corporate excellence, business growth and expansion, and profitable economies.

Enterprise and Supplier Development

Wellspring Global identifies with government objectives towards social transformation, job creation, skills development, technical and business capability and capacity building, small business promotion and sustainability. We have developed a set of robust enterprise promotion strategies which have enabled Wellspring Global to leverage much value to start-ups and existing small businesses across the African continent.

Wellspring Global actively contributes to maximizing the share of local content for all investment packages at all levels by applying proven social development methodologies. These include unbundling of valuable support services to allow local businesses more opportunities to compete as well as facilitating the match making between foreign investors and local businesses.

Wellspring Global focusses on five key areas:

  • Job creation
  • Skills development
  • Capability and capacity building
  • Small Enterprise promotion and expansion
  • Technology and IPR transfer and sustainability
