About Us

Hochland Guesthouse

Our Story

Established in 2013, Hochland Guesthouse is ideally and conveniently situated within the vicinity of the city of Windhoek. It is close enough for the convenience of those doing business in the CBD and far enough to escape the hustles and noise of the CBD. Our variety of 12 rooms, security and privacy makes us the number choice for accommodation of those visiting Windhoek for business, holiday or just for a break from home. The comfort of our guests is our business.
