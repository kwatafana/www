     21 If we visit the challenge's [webpage](https://www.hackthissite.org/missions/realistic/1/index.php)
     22 we see a page. The interesting parts of the page are the 5 sections
     23 of the 5 bands, the bands are ordered the top to bottom with the best
     24 band being on top and the worst being at the bottom of the list.
     25 
     26 Each section contains an HTML form, here is the form for the top band
     27 (Imposing Republic):
     28 
     29 ```html
     30 <form action="v.php" method="get">
     31 	<input type="hidden" name="PHPSESSID" value="abcaeadfc31a5c43b2534bf995c0553f">
     32 	<input type="hidden" name="id" value="4">
     33 	<select name="vote">
     34 		<option value="1">1</option>
     35 		<option value="2">2</option>
     36 		<option value="3">3</option>
     37 		<option value="4">4</option>
     38 		<option value="5">5</option>
     39 	</select>
     40 	<input type="submit" value="vote!">
     41 </form>
     42 ```
     43 The form sends a `GET` request, this means that the form data will be
     44 appended to the URL in key/value pairs, this it looks for the first 
     45 form:
     46 
     47 </v.php?PHPSESSID=abcaeadfc31a5c43b2534bf995c0553f&id=4&vote=1>
     48 
     49 Where PHPSESSID is the session ID, id
